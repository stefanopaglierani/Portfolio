Nature and Technology Can Finally Coexist

Da Vinci Glamping is exploring a new model of tourism where innovation, clean energy and nature work together instead of competing.

This project aims to create an outdoor destination where guests experience the wilderness while surrounded by smart infrastructure, off-grid systems and next-generation hospitality design.

Why This Project Exists

Tourism infrastructure has barely changed in decades. Hotels still dominate the landscape even when travelers are seeking authentic experiences in nature.

Da Vinci Glamping explores a different idea: what if outdoor hospitality could be smarter, lighter on the land, and powered by clean technology?

This project is currently in early development while studying land opportunities in Northern Colorado.
